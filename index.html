The colder wetter months in Melbourne are a great time to grow veggies, and now is an excellent time to plant! Consider cabbage, asian greens like mizuna, tatsoi or pak choi, lettuce, rocket, spinach, carrots, celery, cauliflower, spring onions, leek, onions, radish, turnips and swedes.

Fruit 'n Veggies to plant in springtime!

Spring is an excellent time to develop your veggie patch - tomato, zucchni, eggplant, lettuce - and herbs like basil, coriander, dill and sage as well - you can even keep some of your winter pants going!

Beautiful flowers!

Springtime is perfect for planting the following flowers in Melbourne: Daisies, Carnations, Lavender, Marigolds, Snapdragons, Pansies, Foxgloves and Dahlias.
